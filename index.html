Research

My research connects model interpretation, physical intuition, and reliable artificial intelligence.

Trustworthy AI

RiemannMamba

A physics-inspired state-space model that investigates interpretable internal signals and their relationship with confidence estimation and robustness under corruption.

Scientific Imaging

TST-Mamba

A temporal-spatial-temporal state-space denoiser for transient non-line-of-sight imaging, designed as a plug-and-play preprocessing module for multiple reconstruction pipelines.

Explainable AI

Interpretable Internal Dynamics

Studying how internal representations and latent signals can be interpreted and reused to improve reliability, explainability, and generalization.

Physics-Inspired AI

Physical Principles for Model Design

Exploring how concepts from physics and dynamical systems can provide useful structures and interpretations for modern machine-learning models.
