Scientific Imaging · NLOS · Architecture

TST-Mamba

A temporal-spatial-temporal state-space denoiser designed as a plug-and-play preprocessing module for non-line-of-sight transient imaging and reconstruction. Its architecture views Mamba as a skip-connection-free autoencoder, compressing temporal information into a bottleneck representation and redistributing the resulting global context through spatial modeling and temporal reinjection.

Driver Handover Readiness Estimation via Bio–Vehicle Signal Fusion and Safety–Oriented Re-decision Conference Paper

WooSuk Choi, NaHee Kim, and JiHyun Nam · Summer Annual Conference of IEIE, 2026

A bio–vehicle signal fusion framework that combines physiological state estimation with vehicle-state information to assess driver readiness for control handover in autonomous driving. The study further introduces a safety-oriented re-decision policy to reduce unsafe handover decisions.

Air Purifier Utilizing Tesla Compressor and Cyclone Dust Collector Published

KCI-Indexed Journal Article · Technology and Education · 2024

Je-Yoon Ryu, Hyunu Kim, Hyun-Wook Chung, Na-Hee Kim, Jae-Hyeon An, and Sun-Kon Lee. Technology and Education, Vol. 12, No. 2, pp. 63–72, 2024.

This study developed a filter-less air purifier by combining a Tesla compressor with a cyclone dust collector. Airflow behavior was examined through computational fluid dynamics analysis, and particle-removal performance was evaluated experimentally.
